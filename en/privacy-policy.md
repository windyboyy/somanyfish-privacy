# HeartScore - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 3, 2026**

Welcome to HeartScore (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

## 1. Information Collection and Storage

The App adopts a **local-first** data storage approach. All data you enter is stored locally on your device. The types of information we collect and store include:

- **Contact Information:** Nicknames, avatars, sources, and notes you create for your crushes.
- **Scoring Data:** Scoring templates you create and scoring records for your crushes.
- **Diary Entries:** Dating diaries and interaction records you write.
- **Photos:** Photos you upload and related descriptions.
- **Tags:** Custom tags you create.

**Important:** All the above data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account (if you have enabled iCloud sync). We do not upload this data to our servers and cannot access any of your personal data.

## 2. iCloud Sync

The App supports data synchronization through Apple iCloud. If you enable this feature:

- Your data will be synced across your devices through Apple's CloudKit service.
- Data transmission and storage are protected by Apple's privacy policy.
- We cannot access any data you store in iCloud.
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device settings.

## 3. Subscriptions and Purchases

The App offers in-app subscription services (Pro membership). All purchases and subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store any of your payment information. Subscription management and billing are handled by Apple.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. We do not share your personal data with any third parties.

## 5. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

- All data is stored locally on your device, protected by the device's own security mechanisms.
- iCloud synced data is protected by Apple's encryption and security mechanisms.
- We do not maintain any backend servers, eliminating the risk of server-side data breaches.

## 6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Access and Modify:** You can view and modify all your data within the App at any time.
- **Delete:** You can delete any data within the App at any time, or delete all local data by uninstalling the App.
- **Export:** Your data is stored locally, and you have full ownership of your data.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We recommend that you review this page periodically for the latest privacy practices.
